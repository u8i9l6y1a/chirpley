Chirpley ecosystem
  • The brand can make the payment in any
    currency, whether fiat or crypto. Because of
    the swapping mechanism in the underlying
    layer, any currency would be converted to
    $CHRP in the backend
  • 20% gross margin is taken on each
    transaction out of which 1/5th is sent to a
    burn address directly
    Chirpley has set aside 15 percent of
    tokenomics for marketing purposes. A tiny
    percentage will be distributed every day and
    vested over a four-year period. Influencers
    will be paid in $CHRP automatically to
    promote the Chirpley platform and token,
    ensuring a steady supply of promotion for the
    platform and token. Also, it will unlock the
    future, fully automated and decentralized
    self-sustaining platform
  • Passive rewards through Staking.
    Influencers can choose to lock the $CHRP
    token for 30-60-90-120 etc. days on platform.
    Even in the case the token drops in value, they
    still get the passive rewards. Further, this
    creates the mechanism to hold and not panic
    sell, in case the value of the token drops, if
    ever
  • Staking also gives the holders voting power
    when Chirpley moves onto a DAO model in
    future, as the token of governance. Holders
    can make decisions on various releases and
    further rewards like NFTs etc
